Welcome

Welcome to Biggest Fish! We are a community that have been sharing our love for fishing in the larger Miami area for over 5 years! What started off as a small forum on a deep corner of the internet,through a massive fundraiser have been able to relocate to a physical office for any in-person enquiries!

On this website, you will find where we share our community's biggest catch of the day, as well as our full contact info, and also some merchandise!

We aim to be the biggest host of fishing and game competition in Florida. If you would like to organise or join a competition, please visit the respective section of our website!

As Biggest Fish was originally a small group created on a free domain, we remain to be completely free for discussion and contribution to catch of the day. We also host a number of free competitions. Some competitions require a monetary entry fee.
