Competitions

Here at Biggest Fish we offer competition hosting and finding services to all fishing enthusiasts in the larger Miami area.

Open Competitions:

  • Monthly Catch!

  • Fish Finding

  • Testing Trout

  • Sensing Snapper

  • Biggest Fish Catch of the day

Hosting a competition:

For all enquiries on hosting a competition, please visit the 'contact us section of our website.

Competition hosting pricing:

Prize money will be arranged by host.


Entry fee $0-10: No fee to host.

Entry fee $10-30: 10% fee to host

Entry fee $30+: 25% fee to host


Catering can be arranged per request and at the expense of the host.
